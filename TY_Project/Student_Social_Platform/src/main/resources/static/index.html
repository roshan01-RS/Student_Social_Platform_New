Where Students Connect, Chat & Grow Together

Conify is the ultimate social platform built exclusively for students. Connect with classmates, join study groups, share your journey, and build lasting friendships in one vibrant community designed just for you.

Connect & Share in Your Way

Four powerful features to make your student life amazing

Chat

Connect one-on-one with classmates instantly. Share ideas, ask questions, and build meaningful friendships through real-time conversations.

Group Chat

Create or join group conversations for your courses. Collaborate on projects, study together, and never miss important updates from your study squad.

Community

Join vibrant communities based on your interests, major, or hobbies. Discover events, share resources, and grow your network across campus.

Post

Share your thoughts, achievements, and experiences with your community. Get feedback, celebrate wins, and inspire others with your journey.
